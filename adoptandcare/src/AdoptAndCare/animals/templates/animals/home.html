About

Our site allows free virtual accommodation of stray animals being created especially for animal shelters that need help to donate animals to the right people who have the opportunity to take proper care of them. Anyone who is logged in can post an ad with a donation of any type of animal. All posts are managed by the site administrator. The adopter must contact the owner to establish details related to the adoption of the animal (meeting place, documents if necessary).
